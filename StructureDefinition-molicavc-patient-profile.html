Resource Profile: Profile of Molic-AVC's Patient

Official URL: https://molic-avc.gabriellesantosleandro.com/StructureDefinition/molicavc-patient-profile Version: 1.0.1
Active as of 2023-10-25 Computable Name: MolicAVC_PatientProfile

The Patient Resource of the Molic-AVC Project stores demographics and other administrative information about an individual or animal receiving care or other health-related services.

Usage:

  • Examples for this Resource Profile: Patient/123.456.789-00
