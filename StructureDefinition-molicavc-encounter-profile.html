Resource Profile: Profile of Molic-AVC's Encounter

Official URL: https://molic-avc.gabriellesantosleandro.com/StructureDefinition/molicavc-encounter-profile Version: 1.0.1
Active as of 2023-11-21 Computable Name: MolicAVC_EncounterProfile

The Encounter Resource in the MOLIC-AVC project represents the actual interaction between a patient and their healthcare provider(s). It’s designed to document delivered health services or assess the patient’s health status.

Usage:

  • Examples for this Resource Profile: Encounter/MolicAVC-EncouterExample
